"Le Grotto are one of the sloshiest live acts around. They bring an unconventional twist of surf, grunge and folk to densely packed house shows which tend to smell of beer, pizza and garlic. Their stage presence is most energetic and it honestly exposes a crowd that you don't often see. If you tell yourself you're too old for house shows, cos you might be, you can often catch them at some bar or formal venue in Olympia (sometimes with male burlesque accompaniment). They formed just around Halloween last year, but most musicians in town already know about about em and strive to share a bill. If you do get a chance to hang out with the dudes, they'll probably spare some of their Hamms. If you've heard their music online but haven't seen their live shit you should make that a priority."

Robin A. [2016]
